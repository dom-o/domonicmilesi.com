One Rep Max Calculator and Equations

Sometimes these equations evaulate to something that doesn't really make sense, e.g. negative reps.
Sensible outputs mitigates that as follows:
  -no negative reps; outputs 0 instead.
    --unfortunately some of the formulas will still output ludicrous rep numbers with submaximal weight.
  -when calculating reps: if the weight is more than your 1RM, output 0 reps.
  -when calculating reps: if the weight is your 1RM, output 1 rep.
  -when calculating 1RM: if you do 1 rep with a weight, output that entered weight.
